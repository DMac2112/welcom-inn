Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about partnering with Welcom-Inn.

How do you price my property?

We use a combination of local market knowledge, historical data, and dynamic pricing algorithms to adjust your rates daily. This ensures you're priced competitively to maximize both occupancy and revenue during high and low seasons.

What is your commission rate?

Our commission rate typically ranges from 15% to 22% of the booking revenue, depending on the level of service required. We only get paid when you get paid.

Who handles the cleaning and maintenance?

We work with trusted, professional cleaning contractors who clean the property to a 5-star standard after every guest. They also report any maintenance issues, which we coordinate to fix promptly.

How and when do I get paid?

You will receive a detailed financial report along with your payout directly to your bank account by the 5th of every month for the previous month's bookings.

Can I still use the property myself?

Absolutely! It's your property. You simply let us know the dates you wish to use it, and we will block those out on the calendar (provided they aren't already booked by a guest).

How do you handle difficult guests?

We vet guests according to platform policies, handle all communication 24/7, and manage any issues that arise during their stay. We also handle any damage claims through the booking platforms on your behalf.

Still have questions?

We're here to help. Reach out to our team directly.
